ABOUT US

MKM ENGINEERING

All Types Of Precision Machining Components

MKM ENGINEERING is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company established in 2023, built on over 25 years of hands-on industry experience in the high-precision CNC machining sector.

We specialize in delivering reliable CNC machining services for industries that require accuracy and consistency, including Aerospace, Defense, Medical Implants, Automotive, and Oil & Gas.

Our facility is equipped with CNC Auto Lathes and high-precision turning centers capable of handling both batch and high-volume production. We focus on meeting client requirements through consistent process control, timely delivery, and practical engineering support.

At MKM ENGINEERING, we aim to support our clients as a dependable manufacturing partner. We believe in transparent communication, continuous process improvement, and delivering components that meet specifications without compromise.

We welcome opportunities from businesses that prioritize quality, value long-term collaboration, and seek reliable machining partners.
